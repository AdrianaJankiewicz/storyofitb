Welcome to Logo

You would like to learn more about the logo of Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown? Well, you aren't the only one. How the first logo came to be and what it meant is pretty much a mystery. Maybe, just maybe though, after hours of researching and questioning the staff members, it would be possible to find out more about it... Or maybe it's better to keep it as mystery. Somethings are better left unknown.
What we do know though, is the process that went into creating the second logo, the one that we know and see today. In fact, we even have some pictures from the meeting. Come, come closer, have a look...
