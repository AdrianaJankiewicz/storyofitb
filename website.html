Welcome to the Website


Well, here you are! I thought you got lost... Curious how the website changed over all those years? It changed a lot, I can promise you that. It went from good designs to bad designs to even worse, to finally end up at a decent one. Sit down, get comfortable, I will tell you all about it. First ITB site can be tracked back to 2000 and it followed all the "trendy" things for that year - it had the visitors tracker and even an email link for those who would like to request a Prospectus. Looking at it now, at the end of 2018, it's hard to spot beauty in the seamed background and bright colors. Next came the cyan version of the site, in late 2000. It sported the look that can be seen even now. It was simple, but a little bit empty, but that changed in 2001 when they added in more content.
At 2002 something clearly went wrong. The whole site become weirdly small and the lack of color wasn't helping either. And this website lasted till 2003! They added in some colors - vibrant reds and oranges and completely got rid of the logo. Then, in August, the usuable area shrinked even further, but at least some colors had been added to the palette. And a black and white photograph, which is lovely, actually.
In 2004 came the era of blue, even the logo changed colors. The drop-shadow, common at the time, was also used... But then it all changed again in 2005. Blue was forgotten and grey colors took over the palette once again. Logo was slightly changed again, this time to look like a ghost of the previous versions... And some Irish was added in to the mix. And it stayed this way for a long, log time.
In 2009, the green colors dominated. Green, green everywhere. The logo had been completely redesigned to something that looks like falling dominos. And the funny thing is... It stayed this way till 2018. Nothing had changed since then, as if they fired their web developer... Maybe they did?
Anyway, anyway. Hope you liked this quick back-in-time travelling experience. Now, go look for more information on the other pages...
