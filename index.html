Welcome to the Story of ITB

It's good to see someone here... Someone interesting in history, perhaps? More in ecology? Well, if so, you are in a good place. Ah, but you only got lost? Well, well. Head over to official ITB website if that's what you are looking for. Farewell!
But since some of you stayed, let me go through the purpose of this website. It's pretty simple, really. It is made to inform you, the viewer, about the journey of Institute of Technology, Blanchardstown, as this place will soon stop to exist under this name and will become TU Dublin. This adventure started many years ago, in 1999, on fields in the middle of nowhere. Soon later, a first building emerged and many young people gave it a chance - chance to grow, to educate and to become known. Those people flooded the front doors in hopes of decent education and possibilities. Some got what they wanted, some didn't. Some stayed, some left in order to find what they were looking for.
From there, the college bloomed. More buildings were introducted to accomodate ever-coming students and staff members. And till this day, it's growing bigger and stronger... but now, instead of functioning on its own, it will merge with two other colleges... We shall see how the future will unfold.

Purpose of Website

This website was made to educate and inform people about the story of ITB and the surrounding area. It is written in a narrative form to make it more interesting and less like a wikipedia-style website. It's also made for an assignment for Second Year Web Design Module.
