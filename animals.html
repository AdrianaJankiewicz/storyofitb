Welcome to the Animals

ITB is full of greenery and wood areas, so it naturally is a home to a good few animals. Sometimes, when you are especially lucky, you can spot some of the cutest wild rabbits. Tiny, fluffy balls with medium-lenght ears and that ball tail... But then again, that is rare.
You might have a bit more luck seeing one or two of the local cats that seem to live in the area too. If you try to walk up to them though, they will run away, and then stop, give you a weird look and continue running.
Some rumours say that ITB's area is also home to foxes, but those aren't too social and prefer not to show their beautiful ginger fur to the public.

Then there are birds. The standard birds that you see everywhere, I may add, so it's nothing uncommon... Unless you get the privalage to spot a Buzzard. In the nearby lake, you can spot some swans, ducks and water fowls, just make sure to bring them some food, otherwise they will try to eat you.
